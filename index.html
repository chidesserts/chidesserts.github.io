CHI DESSERTS

small cake business located in rosemead, california

ABOUT US

Chi Desserts is at-home baking business located in Rosemead, California, and our mission to to spread love and hope during this quarantine. We currently sell bento cakes, brownies, and cookies. All our packaging are either biodegradable or sustainable for the environment. We offer free pickup and free delivery within a 6-mile radius of Rosemead (a $3 delivery fee will be charged if it's farther than that). Acceptable payment methods include Venmo, Paypal, and cash.

OUR STORY

Chi Desserts was founded by Jacqueline Chen in 2020. She launched this small business during quarantine when she realized her passion to bake and gift. During quarantine, she would spend the majority of her time in the kitchen baking her goods and out delivering those goods to her friends. Chen saw how her baked goods could light up the smiles of her friends and decided to develop her passion into an at-home business.
